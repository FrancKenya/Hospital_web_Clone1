Our Services

In patient:

Neema hospital has a general ward, an executive suite and exclusive paediatric ward for children. The rooms are well designed to provide comfort and not to cause any infections and reinfections to our patients. Our infrastructure gives the optimum environment to facilitate recupwration

We have an Intensive care unit which is a state of the art 30 bed facility

Out patient

Ambulance services

24 hour ambulance services: We have an around the clock ambulance system that is available within institutions and on the road. The service is available to everyone within Nairobi and can be contacted through calling our toll free number provided in the contact section

24 hour laboratory services

Neema hospital has state of the art diagnostic equipments and well experienced personnel to carry out laboratory services. We provide a 24 hour services system whereby you can drop by, and get your result anytime

Besides that we carry dentistry, accident and emergency services, dialysis, hydrotherapy, paediatric, pharmacy,and plastic surgery
